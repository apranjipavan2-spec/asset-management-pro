👔 Asset Administrator Guide

Operations & Control

Welcome to Kalike Asset Management

As an Asset Administrator (Manager), you oversee asset allocation, approve employee requests, manage asset transfers, and monitor maintenance operations. You have control over your team's asset inventory and workflow approvals.

Your Dashboard Overview

📊 Dashboard shows:
  • Team asset allocation summary
  • Pending asset requests from your team
  • Asset transfers and movements
  • Maintenance requests
  • Depreciation tracking
  • Key metrics and reports

Core Features

1️⃣ Manage Asset Registry

Master Asset Registry - Full Control:
  • View all company assets (complete inventory)
  • Add new assets to the system
  • Update asset details (location, condition, custodian)
  • Mark assets as active/inactive
  • Track asset lifecycle (acquisition → depreciation → disposal)

2️⃣ Approve Asset Requests

Review and Approve/Reject Employee Requests:
  1. View all pending requests from your team
  2. Review justification and business need
  3. Approve to authorize procurement
  4. Reject with feedback if not needed
  5. Track approval history
💡 Tip: Approvals trigger procurement workflow. Rejected requests can be resubmitted.

3️⃣ Allocate Assets to Team Members

Assign assets from inventory to specific employees. You can:

  • Manually allocate assets to employees
  • Bulk assign assets to multiple team members
  • Generate allocation reports
  • Track who has what assets

4️⃣ Manage Transfers

Asset Transfers Between Employees:
  • Approve inter-team asset transfers
  • Manage asset movement tracking
  • Track transfer history
  • Ensure proper chain of custody

5️⃣ Maintenance & Issues

Oversee asset maintenance operations:

  • View all maintenance requests from your team
  • Assign to maintenance team
  • Track repair status
  • Monitor asset health

6️⃣ View Team Data

Your Team Visibility:

  • 📋 See all team member tasks and worklogs
  • 📊 View team performance reviews
  • 📅 Access team leave balances
  • 🎯 Monitor team productivity
💡 As a manager, you automatically see all data for your direct reports (users where reportsTo = your ID).

Request Approval Workflow

Procurement Fulfills Monitor progress Asset acquired
You Allocate Assign to employee Employee receives asset
You Reject Click "Reject" + reason Employee notified, can resubmit

Best Practices

  • ✅ Approve requests promptly (within 24 hours ideally)
  • ✅ Provide feedback on rejected requests
  • ✅ Regularly audit your team's assets
  • ✅ Monitor asset condition reports
  • ✅ Track depreciation for financial planning
  • ✅ Ensure proper documentation of transfers

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I reassign assets without employee request?

A: Yes, as a manager you can manually allocate/reallocate assets directly.

Q: How do I handle lost or damaged assets?

A: Mark the asset condition as "Poor" or use the Issues feature. Finance will handle write-offs.

Q: Can I see depreciation values?

A: Yes, depreciation is calculated automatically and visible in asset details.

Q: How do I bulk assign assets?

A: Use the "Bulk Actions" feature in the Asset Registry to assign multiple items at once.

Q: What data can I see about my team?

A: Tasks, worklogs, reviews, and leave balances for all employees reporting to you (reportsTo field).

🛒 Request Assets

▼

Need equipment? Submit a request to your manager for approval.

Steps:

  1. Navigate to Request section
  2. Select asset category
  3. Pick the specific asset
  4. Add justification (why you need it)
  5. Submit for manager review

Tip: Be clear and specific in your justification to speed up approvals.

📦 Manage Your Assets

▼

View all equipment assigned to you with complete details.

What You'll See:

  • Asset name and category
  • Serial number and condition
  • Current location and assignment date
  • Maintenance history
  • Transfer/return options

Important: Keep track of your assets and report any issues immediately.

⚠️ Report Issues

▼

Found damage, malfunction, or loss? Report it immediately to the maintenance team for repair or investigation.

How to Report:

  1. Go to Issues/Maintenance section
  2. Select the affected asset
  3. Describe the problem clearly
  4. Attach photos if possible
  5. Submit issue ticket

Note: Early reporting helps with repairs and prevents further damage.

🔄 Request Asset Transfers

▼

Need to move an asset to a different location or project? You can now submit transfer requests directly through the system.

When to Request a Transfer:

  • Asset needs to move to a different department
  • Project completion requires asset relocation
  • Equipment is being reassigned to another team member
  • Temporary asset loan to colleague (with approval)

How to Request a Transfer:

  1. Go to Request New Asset section
  2. Click the "Transfer Asset" tab
  3. Select the asset you want to transfer from your active assets
  4. Provide the reason for the transfer
  5. Click "Request Transfer" to submit

What Happens Next:

  • Your transfer request is sent to your manager
  • Manager reviews and approves/rejects the transfer
  • You receive notification of the decision
  • Once approved, the transfer is processed immediately

Important: Never transfer assets without approval from your manager.

Request Workflow & Statuses

▼

Understand what each request status means:

Status Meaning Next Step
✏️ Draft Still being edited by you Complete & submit when ready
⏳ Pending Waiting for manager approval Wait for review
✅ Approved Approved — asset being acquired Wait for allocation
❌ Rejected Not approved by manager Ask manager for feedback; revise & resubmit
📦 Allocated Ready to collect or in your inventory Collect or begin using

Your Responsibilities

▼
  • Request Appropriately: Request only what you genuinely need for your work
  • Maintain Assets: Keep assigned assets in good working condition
  • Report Issues: Report damage, loss, or malfunction immediately
  • Return When Asked: Return assets promptly when requested by management
  • No Unauthorized Transfers: Don't share or transfer assets without approval
  • Inventory Checks: Participate in periodic inventory verifications
  • Use Appropriately: Use assets only for work-related purposes

Quick Answers (FAQ)

▼

How long does approval take?

Usually 1-2 business days. Be clear and specific with your justification to speed it up.

My request was rejected, what now?

Contact your manager to understand why. They can provide feedback for revision. Once you address the concerns, you can resubmit the request.

I damaged an asset, what should I do?

Report it in the Issues section right away with a photo if possible. This helps with assessment and repair planning. Don't hide the damage.

Can I transfer an asset to a colleague?

Yes! You can now submit a transfer request directly through the "Transfer Asset" tab in the Request section. Your manager will review and approve the transfer. Direct transfers without authorization are not permitted.

Need help with a request or issue?

Contact your manager for request-related questions or the asset/maintenance team for technical issues.

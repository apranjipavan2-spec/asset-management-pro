👤 Employee Portal Guide

Asset Requisitioning & Management

Welcome to Kalike Asset Management

As an employee, this portal allows you to request assets, track your allocations, and manage equipment issued to you. Your role focuses on asset requisitioning and personal asset management.

Your Dashboard Overview

📊 Dashboard: Your home page shows:
  • Assets allocated to you
  • Pending asset requests
  • Recent approvals/rejections
  • Asset condition status

Core Features

1️⃣ Request Assets

How to Request an Asset:
  1. Click "Request" in the sidebar
  2. Select the asset category (equipment, furniture, software, etc.)
  3. Choose the specific asset you need
  4. Specify the reason/justification
  5. Submit for approval
💡 Tip: Requests go to your manager for approval. Provide clear justification for faster approvals.

2️⃣ View Your Assets

All assets allocated to you are visible in your personal asset list. You can:

  • View asset details (serial number, purchase date, condition)
  • Check asset location and custody information
  • Report issues or damage
  • Request asset transfer/replacement

3️⃣ Report Issues

If an asset is damaged or needs maintenance:
  1. Click "Issues" in your asset details
  2. Describe the problem
  3. Attach photos if possible
  4. Submit to maintenance team

4️⃣ Request Transfers

Need to transfer an asset to another employee? Your manager can approve inter-employee transfers.

5️⃣ Track Notifications

  • 🔔 Request approvals/rejections
  • 🔔 Asset allocation updates
  • 🔔 Maintenance notifications
  • 🔔 Announcements from management

Request Workflow

Status Meaning Next Step
✏️ Draft You're still editing the request Complete and submit
⏳ Pending Awaiting manager review Wait for approval/rejection
✅ Approved Manager approved, procurement will fulfill Asset will arrive soon
❌ Rejected Request denied Revise and resubmit if needed
📦 Allocated Asset assigned to you Asset is now in your inventory

Asset Categories

You can request assets from these categories:

  • 💻 IT Equipment (laptops, monitors, peripherals)
  • 🪑 Furniture (desk, chair, cabinet)
  • 📞 Communication Devices (phones, headsets)
  • 📚 Software Licenses
  • 🛠️ Tools & Equipment
  • 📱 Mobile Devices

Important Rules

⚠️ Please Remember:
  • Only request what you actually need
  • Take care of company assets — you're responsible for them
  • Report damage or loss immediately
  • Return assets when requested or when leaving the company
  • Don't transfer assets without approval

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does approval take?

A: Usually 1-2 business days. Urgent requests can be prioritized.

Q: Can I request multiple assets at once?

A: Yes, but submit separate requests for better tracking.

Q: What if my request is rejected?

A: Contact your manager to understand the reason, then modify and resubmit.

Q: How do I report a lost asset?

A: Use the "Issues" feature to report loss immediately to your manager and asset team.

Q: Can I see the depreciation value of my assets?

A: Yes, depreciation information is visible in the asset details page.

Getting Help

  • 💬 Contact your manager for request approvals
  • 🔧 Asset team handles maintenance and repairs
  • 📧 Finance team manages depreciation and valuations
  • ❓ Help section in the app (or contact IT)
